Privacy Policy for Aqua Tag

Last updated: September 09, 2024

Welcome to Aqua Tag. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Aqua Tag is designed to edit images locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or edited images to our servers. The app requires access to your device's storage to read and save images, and camera access if you choose to take new photos within the app.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use your data for any purpose beyond the functionality provided within the app on your device.

Data Storage and Security

All images and edits are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to these files. The security of your data is dependent on your device's security settings.

Third-Party Services

Aqua Tag does not use any third-party services that collect information about you.

Children's Privacy

Aqua Tag is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
